Nativity Advent Calendar

I had an aggreement with the professor of this class. The agreement was to use a student, Kylia, outside the class as a client and build a website for her hobby, and I would just be the solo developer on this assignment. I did not get the experience of working with a team, but I did get the experience of using everything I learned in class to build this website.

Client Report

How did you decide what content and images to use for your site plan?

She has a hobby, and I offered to turn it into a website.

How did you communicate with your developer? Were there any issues with communication?

I communicated over Microsoft Teams. She had little time to provide me with content and feedback, but she was able to provide me with all the content I needed on time.

Did your developer meet your expectations for your site?

I met her expectations and went beyond. She's very happy with the outcome.

What will you do differently next time you hire a team to do a project for you?

She may provide more feedback and more intentional content rather than coming up with things on the fly.

Lead Developer Report

How did you manage your development team and communicate the tasks to your junior dev?

There was not team, but if I had to communicate with one, it would have been over Microsoft Teams. Managing a team would be to create deadlines for them so I could continue to make progress by the time the deadline occured.

How did you communicate with your client? Were there any issues?

As mentioned, it was by Microsoft Teams, and the issues were not receiving content right away due to her busy schedule.

How did you handle publishing your client's site and sharing it with the client?

I did not do anything beyond utilizing Github's webhosting. The client received a link directly to the home page so she was able to view it as published.

How was your experience with managing your site with GitHub?

The experience was flawless. The time for updating the website was always really quick. Never had an issue, and having record of every update made me feel safe if anything were to happen.

Junior Developer Report

How did you communicate with your team lead? Were there any issues?

I was the team lead.

Did you know what was expected of you for the site?

The child pages were using most of the css from the styles file, and only needed a little section at the bottom to organize the content without changing the home page.

Were you able to contribute to your site?

No issues.

Did you run into any issues with GitHub and adding your changes to the site?

I still have yet to see what it's like to manage seperate branches from a team and decide which one to push to the master.

General Experience and Reflections

What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?

The most challenging was making the whole box in a table to be a clickable link. I did no overcome this because I decided that it would be better to have text within the box able to be highlighted if someone wanted to copy-paste something from the box without being forced to follow the link. They could from the page the link leads to, but I settled and called it quality-of-life.

What did you enjoy most about this project?

I enjoyed making a friend happy. This experience gave me the motivation to do this for her sooner than later, but with the quality needed to have for a good final assignment in this class. I also really enjoyed seeing my efforts come to life as I saw the CSS code I wrote actually working as I intended.

What would you do differently if you could do the project again?

I would see what working with a teammate might be like. I would give someone the opportunity to be a junior developer for this site, and make changes to my work based on what I liked the junior developer did.

Do you feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience? Explain.

I feel very confident. I was going to do this project regardless. I know I can make good looking websites and as a creator, I can focus on quality-of-life features. After doing this, I have proven to myself I can make something truly fantastic.
